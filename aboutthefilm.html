About Eyes Open

Film Description

This is not about how thievery is bad nor it is for normalizing it. It is not about villains and heroes, or about victims and their stories. It is all about what really happens in the end.

But this is a story with a beginning and a middle, with no end.

Production by Cineast

Cineast is an Abu Dhabi - based production and distribution company with a core team of directors, filmmakers, graphic designers, , engineers, vfx specialists, 3D artists and animators. We combine these skills to meet any brief and craft award winning work.

Release

Eyes Open had its world premiere at the 30 Minute Short Film Festival on September 8, 2020. The film was theatrically released in the United Arab Emirates on September 19, 2020, distributed by Cineast Productions. The film is digitally available through the main website starting from September 29, 2020.
